Our Story

A Family Legacy of Sweetness

DADDY's Bakery was born from a father's love for baking — every recipe perfected over decades, every product crafted with genuine care. What started as weekend bakes for the family has grown into Virar's most beloved artisan bakery.

We use only the finest ingredients — from premium Belgian chocolate to hand-milled flours — because every bite you take deserves to be extraordinary.
